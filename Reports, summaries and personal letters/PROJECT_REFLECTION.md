### English Version
This project has been a passion of mine for a long time. I had a strong desire to understand mobile security deeply and to challenge myself by compromising a mobile device in a controlled environment.

This journey was a great pleasure for me. I experienced difficulties and technical hurdles all along the way, but I persisted, researched, and learned. In the end, I believe I have gained a real understanding of this complex landscape.

For me, this was not just a project for a grade, but a long-term mission I felt the need to conquer. Dealing with building the server, coding the application, and executing the manual exploits taught me more than any theoretical course could.

**Jesse Antman**
